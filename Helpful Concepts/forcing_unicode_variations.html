Forcing Unicode Variations

To retain the appearance of unicode characters in one form or another, you can append an additional variation selector that indicates to the rendering engine how the character should be displayed.

Variation-Selector-15: U+FE0E

↩︎

Variation-Selector-16: U+FE0F

↩️

Nota Bene:

As always, Outlook doesn't support these characters and will display them as a missing character. Use your outlook-hide class to keep forced unicode characters from displaying.
